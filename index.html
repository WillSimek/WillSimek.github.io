Circut Of The Americas


The first modern day track to be raced by F1 in the US, introduced in 2012.

Miami Grand Prix


A very new track introduced in 2022, after the boom in popularity of F1 over covid and the netflix show releasing.

Los Vegas Grand Prix


Another new track introduced to be raced in 2023, because of the increasing popularity of F1 racing in America.
